Path Converter.

Convert windows to linux style paths or vice versa.

Usage:
  pc [-w] <path>
  pc -c [-w] [<path>]
  pc (-h | --help)

Options:
  -h, --help       Show this screen.
  -c, --clipboard  Copy output to clipboard. 
                   Also copies from clipboard when <path> is omitted.
  -w, --windows    Convert to windows style path instead of linux style.
